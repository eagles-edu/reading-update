5. Ask Her Out


Repeat
  

A: I'm in love with that girl.
     B: Have you told her?
A: Of course not.
     B: Why not?
A: She would laugh at me.
     B: How do you know?
A: Because they always do.
     B: Maybe she's different.
A: They're all the same.
     B: Just ask her out to dinner.
A: And then what?
     B: And then she'll know that you like her.
