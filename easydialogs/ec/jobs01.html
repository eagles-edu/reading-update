1. I Need a Job


Repeat
  

A: I need a job.
     B: I thought you had a job.
A: I did.
     B: What happened?
A: I got laid off.
     B: That's terrible! When did it happen?
A: I got laid off last week.
     B: Just you?
A: No, ten of my coworkers got laid off, too.
     B: What are you going to do?
A: I'm looking in the newspaper for a job.
     B: Good luck!
