21. God Is Watching


Repeat

     B: So?
A: You know what that means.
     B: I forgot.
A: Sunday means we go to church.
     B: Oh, yeah.
A: Put on a coat and tie.
     B: Why?
A: To show respect to God and others.
     B: I'm glad Sunday is only once a week.
A: I hope God didn't hear that.
     B: He'll forgive me
